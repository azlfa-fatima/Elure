About Us

GLAMOUR is an independent teen magazine dedicated to celebrating creativity, expression, and voices of our generation. From art and fiction to fashion, mental health, and world issues — every page reflects the spark and struggles of youth today.

Started by young writers and dreamers, our magazine provides a platform where teens from around the world can contribute their poetry, essays, photography, and stories. We believe that every teen has a story worth telling.

Each monthly issue is a mirror of teen life — unfiltered, bold, and deeply authentic. Join us as we create a space that’s not only about reading, but about belonging.

We Work On

Youth Voice

We believe in the power of storytelling. Whether it’s poetry, essays, or personal reflections, our magazine amplifies the voices of teens worldwide. Here, every story counts — the loud, the quiet, the bold, and the unspoken.

Wellness & Identity

Growing up is messy, beautiful, and sometimes overwhelming. That’s why we dedicate space to mental health, self-discovery, and personal growth. From navigating school pressure to embracing identity, our wellness section is a safe corner for honesty and healing.

Entertainment & Culture

From music that moves us to shows that define us, we dive into what’s trending — and what’s timeless. Expect thoughtful reviews, interviews with rising teen creators, and fresh takes on the pop culture shaping our world.

Fashion & Creativity

Style is expression. Art is rebellion. Creativity is freedom. GLAMOUR showcases teen designers, artists, and photographers who dare to push boundaries. Every page is a canvas, celebrating originality and individuality.

Poetry

Words that breathe life. Our poetry section captures raw emotions, fleeting moments, and the rhythm of teenhood — unfiltered and deeply personal.

Photography

A picture tells a thousand stories. From portraits to street captures, GLAMOUR showcases the world through the lens of young creatives.

Why We Exist

We are teens, writing for teens. Independent, unfiltered, and unapologetically authentic — this is our space to belong, create, and inspire. Glimmer of Teens isn’t just a magazine you read; it’s a magazine you can be part of.
