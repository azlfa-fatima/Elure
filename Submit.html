Submit Your Work

At GLAMOUR, we welcome submissions from young creatives across the globe. Whether you are a writer, artist, or photographer, this is your platform to share your voice and creativity.

What We Accept

  • Poetry, Short Fiction, and Creative Writing
  • Articles and Essays on youth, wellness, identity, culture, and world issues
  • Entertainment Writing: reviews, interviews, and opinion pieces
  • Art and Illustrations (JPEG/PNG, high resolution)
  • Photography (color, black & white, or creative edits)
  • Fashion & Creative Styling spreads

How to Submit

  1. Prepare your work in a clean document (Word, PDF, or JPG/PNG for visual work).
  2. Include your name, age, country, and a short bio (2–3 sentences).
  3. Attach your files and send them to: writerazlfafatima@gmail.com
  4. Use the subject line: Submission – [Your Name] – [Category]

By submitting, you give us permission to feature your work in our monthly magazine and on our online platforms. All rights remain with you, the original creator.
